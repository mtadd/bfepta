What: Advocacy Presentation and Discussion
Who will be presenting: Wake County PTA Council and Public Schools First NC
When:Monday, 10/16, from 7-8pm
Where: Brassfield Road Elementary School Media Center
For: Brassfield, Baileywick, and Wakefield ES parents and area PTA Boards.

Please RSVP to attend here.

The North Carolina Legislature passed an unfunded K-3 class size mandate last spring which will take full effect in 2018-2019. Join us on October 16 to hear about the mandate, how it will affect Wake County Public Schools, and the possible solutions offered by the school district to deal with the new requirements. We will also discuss what PTA units, leaders, members and parents can do to advocate for our students and teachers.

Come join us to be part of an open discussion of strategy ideas to affect change! Questions? Tania
